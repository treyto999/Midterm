Not as important as breathing but still important for long range shooting.

Grip is important.

Having a grip thats not to tight or to light.
You want to have a grip that is comfortable but still firm to stay on target.
If it's to tight you will pull of target and you will miss your mark.
If it's to light you will go off target as soon as you fire cause you won't be held on your target well.
If your grip is just right you will have zero problems and hit your mark.
